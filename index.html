Purpose: Eliminate redundancy and improve data integrity by organizing data into well-structured tables.

ACID ensures database transactions are reliable and maintain data integrity:
1. Atomicity: Transaction is "all or nothing"â€”if one part fails, entire transaction is rolled back. No partial updates.
